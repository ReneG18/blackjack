Casino Style
- 3 deck of cards
- first to 21 wins
- bust if over 21
- house and player have the same amount than its a tie players keeps their bet

Dealer Rules
- Must hit on all 17s
- "Dealer hits soft 17": This is more common today and slightly favors the house. If the dealer has an Ace and a six (Soft 17), they must hit. They only stand once they reach a "hard" 17 or any total of 18 or higher.
- No Doubling Down: The dealer can never double their bet.
- No Splitting: The dealer cannot split pairs.
- Must Play to the Total: The dealer cannot stop at 16, even if every player at the table has already busted. They must play their hand out until they reach the required total or bust.
